Healthy living

Fat or not, keep your body healthy by doing the following things:

Healthy Eating

Even though you look fat, it doesn't mean you have to eat less. Adequate nutritional intake is needed by the body to grow, develop and carry out optimal activities.

Nutritional balance of food intake is necessary. Apart from that, also pay attention to food processing, don't damage the nutrients in it. Make sure you still get enough nutritional intake, okay?

Regular exercise

Correct exercise at the right time can improve blood circulation and strengthen muscles and bones. Do at least 150 minutes of exercise every week. Make sure not to exercise during your body's rest hours, such as 11 or 12 at night. Appropriate training intervals are also needed so that the body has time to recover from fatigue.

Manage stress

Psychology can affect your health, you know. Make sure you manage stress well, okay? This can be done through recreation, hobbies, yoga or meditation.

Rest

Getting enough rest is important for your body so that it can recover naturally. Make sure you get enough sleep at night because the quality of night sleep is better than morning sleep (don't stay up late and then go to bed early, OK)
